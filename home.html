Health Tips

  • What to Do in Case of Choking (Heimlich Maneuver)


    For Adults & Children (Above 1 Year):

    • Stand behind the choking person and wrap your arms around their waist.
    • Make a fist with one hand and place it just above the navel.
    • Grasp the fist with your other hand and give quick, inward and upward thrusts.
    • Repeat until the object is expelled.
